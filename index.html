Metro 2033 Redux is based on the novel Metro 2033 by Russian author Dmitry Glukhovsky, and closely follows its story, characters, and setting while adding interactive elements unique to the video game format..

ARTYOM IS CAPTURED BY THE RED LINE, A COMMUNIST FACTION IN THE METRO. HE ESCAPES WITH THE HELP OF A MAN NAMED BOURBON, CONTINUING HIS JOURNEY THROUGH HOSTILE TERRITORY.
ARTYOM HELPS DEFEND A STATION FROM A WAVE OF MUTANTS, SHOWING THE CONSTANT THREAT THEY POSE. THE CHAOS AND LOSS HERE UNDERLINE THE STAKES OF HUMAN SURVIVAL IN THE METRO.
ARTYOM CLIMBS AN OLD RADIO TOWER TO GUIDE MISSILES TO THE DARK ONES’ HIVE. IN THE FINAL MOMENTS, THE PLAYER’S CHOICES DETERMINE WHETHER HE FOLLOWS THROUGH WITH THE STRIKE OR CHOOSES PEACE.
